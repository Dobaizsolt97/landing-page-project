Wellcome, young wizzard

    Go ahhead and choose the house you want to join

    Gryffindor

    Loyal, brave, courageous, daring, adventurous, chivalrous
    – those are the traits of a true Gryffindor

    Hufflepuff

    Loyalty, dedication, patience, fair play, and hard work
    – those are the qualities of a Hufflepuff

    Ravenclaw

    Knowledge, creativity, intelligence, wisdom, and cleverness
    – those are the qualities of a Ravenclaw.

    Slytherin

    Intelligent, determined, cunning, ambitious, resourceful, and prideful
    – those are the qualities of a Slytherin.
